Who We Are

Founded on a dream and driven by compassion, Hugs Cafe is more than a restaurant — it's a movement.

🎯

Our Mission

To enhance the lives of individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities through training and employment.

🔭

Our Vision

We envision a world where people are recognized for the things they can do rather than the things they cannot.

💛

Our Belief

We believe in offering Hope, Understanding, Grace, and Success (HUGS) to each individual so they can be recognized for their talents in the community.

What HUGS Stands For

Four core values guide everything we do — from the kitchen to the community.

H
🌟

Hope

We nurture the belief that every individual can achieve great things, lighting a path toward independence and purpose.

U
🤝

Understanding

We cultivate empathy and patience, meeting each person where they are and celebrating their unique journey.

G
🌿

Grace

We create a compassionate environment where mistakes become lessons and every effort is honored with kindness.

S
🏆

Success

We define success by the growth, confidence, and joy our team members discover through meaningful work each day.

Our Programs

Four distinct programs working together to build skills, confidence, and community for adults with IDD.

🍽️

Hugs Cafe

Est. 2015

Our flagship program employing individuals with IDD in a full-service cafe setting — from greeting guests to preparing world-class food.

🌱

Hugs Greenhouse

Est. 2018

Our team grows native Texas perennials and seasonal bedding plants on 4 acres, learning gardening, retail, and socialization skills.

🎓

Hugs Training Academy

Est. 2022

A 12-week workforce readiness course teaching culinary and hospitality skills, including the Food Handler's Exam and job placement.

👨‍🍳

Hugs Home Cooking

Est. 2019

Monthly group cooking classes led by a classically trained chef, building life skills, independence, and social connections.
